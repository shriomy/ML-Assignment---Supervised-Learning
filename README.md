
# ML Assignment - Supervised Learning

## Overview
In this repository, we will be comparing 4 different alogorithms against a common dataset and input variables in a regression problem of predicting annual medical insurance cost of a person.

## Contents
- Data preprocessing
- Model training and evaluation
- Performance metrics
- Results and analysis

## Getting Started
1. Clone this repository
2. Install required dependencies in each branch

## Dataset contains following features: 
1. person_id: Unique identifier for each individual
2. age: Age of the individual (18–90 years)
3. sex: Gender (Male/Female)
4. region: Geographic region
5. urban_rural: Whether the person lives in an urban or rural area
6. income: Annual income (USD)
7. education: Education attainment
8. marital_status: Marital status
9. employment_status: Employment type/status
10. household_size: Number of people in the household
11. dependents: Number of financial dependents
12. bmi: Body Mass Index value
13. smoker: Smoking status (Yes/No)
14. alcohol_freq: Frequency of alcohol consumption
15. visits_last_year: Number of healthcare provider visits in the last year
16. hospitalizations_last_3yrs: Number of hospitalizations in the past three years
17. days_hospitalized_last_3yrs: Total days spent in hospital over the past three years
18. medication_count: Number of active medications prescribed
19. systolic_bp: Systolic blood pressure (mmHg)
20. diastolic_bp: Diastolic blood pressure (mmHg)
21. ldl: LDL cholesterol level (mg/dL)
22. hba1c: Hemoglobin A1c percentage (indicator of blood sugar control)
23. plan_type: Type of insurance plan (e.g., HMO, PPO, EPO)
24. network_tier: Insurance network tier (Basic, Silver, Gold, Platinum)
25. deductible: Annual deductible amount in USD
26. copay: Copayment amount per healthcare service
27. policy_term_years: Length of the insurance policy in years
28. policy_changes_last_2yrs: Number of times the policy was modified in the past two years
29. provider_quality: Quality rating of the primary care provider/insurer
30. risk_score: Calculated health risk score (normalized)
31. annual_medical_cost: Total annual medical cost in USD
32. annual_premium: Annual insurance premium in USD
33. monthly_premium: Monthly insurance premium in USD
34. claims_count: Number of insurance claims filed in a year
35. avg_claim_amount: Average amount per claim in USD
36. total_claims_paid: Total value of claims paid in USD
37. chronic_count: Number of chronic conditions diagnosed
38. hypertension: Indicator for diagnosed hypertension (0 = No, 1 = Yes)
39. diabetes: Indicator for diabetes diagnosis (0 = No, 1 = Yes)
40. asthma: Indicator for asthma diagnosis (0 = No, 1 = Yes)
41. copd: Indicator for Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (0 = No, 1 = Yes)
42. cardiovascular_disease: Presence of cardiovascular disease (0 = No, 1 = Yes)
43. cancer_history: History of cancer diagnosis (0 = No, 1 = Yes)
44. kidney_disease: Indicator for chronic kidney disease (0 = No, 1 = Yes)
45. liver_disease: Indicator for liver disease (0 = No, 1 = Yes)
46. arthritis: Indicator for arthritis diagnosis (0 = No, 1 = Yes)
47. mental_health: Presence of a diagnosed mental health condition (0 = No, 1 = Yes)
48. proc_imaging_count: Number of imaging procedures (e.g., X-ray, MRI, CT scans) performed in a year
49. proc_surgery_count: Number of surgical procedures undergone
50. proc_physio_count: Number of psychiatric/psychological procedures or sessions
51. proc_consult_count: Number of specialist consultations (e.g., cardiologist, oncologist)
52. proc_lab_count: Number of laboratory diagnostic tests taken
53. is_high_risk: Binary indicator if the individual is classified as high health risk (0 = No, 1 = Yes)
